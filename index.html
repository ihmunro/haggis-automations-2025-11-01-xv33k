About Haggis Automations

Haggis Automations was founded with a simple mission: to democratize web presence and make professional website creation accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical skills or budget. Born from the passion of a dedicated local team, we recognized that too many talented entrepreneurs and small business owners were being priced out of having an online presence by expensive hosting providers and complicated website builders. We decided to change that. Starting as a small project in our local community, we've grown into a trusted platform serving thousands of users who have successfully launched their dreams online. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has remained unwavering from day one, driving us to continuously improve and expand our offerings.

At the heart of Haggis Automations are our core values: we believe in transparency, community, and empowerment. We're not just building a website platform; we're building a movement to give power back to the people. Every feature we develop, every decision we make, and every interaction we have with our users is guided by these principles. We're deeply committed to supporting our local community, maintaining the highest standards of security and reliability, and creating an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome and supported. Our vision is to become the world's most trusted website platform, known not just for our free services but for our genuine care for our users' success and our unwavering dedication to making the web more accessible to all.
